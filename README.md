## 🌟 About The Project

**The Youth Contest Beyond Vision** is a comprehensive web platform dedicated to empowering Rwandan youth through debate, public speaking, and leadership development programs. The website serves as a digital hub for information, engagement, and community building around youth empowerment initiatives.

<div align="center">
  <img src="Media/screenshot-home.png" alt="Homepage Screenshot" width="800" style="border-radius: 10px; box-shadow: 0 10px 30px rgba(0,0,0,0.2);"/>
  
  *Homepage - Welcome Modal*
</div>

### 🎯 Our Mission
> Empowering youth through debates, public speaking tournaments, conferences, leadership forums, talk shows, and civic campaigns that cultivate critical thinking, articulate expression, and responsible leadership.

### 👁️ Our Vision
> To build a generation of intellectually fearless, ethically grounded, and globally competent young leaders who shape ideas and drive sustainable transformation.
