Our Projects

Core Youth Empowerment Projects

Our projects are designed to strengthen debate culture, public speaking, leadership development and peaceful civic engagement among secondary school students in Rwanda.

Student Debate Training Project

This project equips secondary school students with debate fundamentals, critical thinking skills, structured argumentation, research techniques, and ethical public discourse. Training sessions are conducted directly in schools, ensuring accessibility and inclusivity.

Public Speaking & Communication Project

Focused on confidence-building and effective expression, this project empowers students to speak clearly, persuasively and responsibly in academic, civic and leadership spaces. It nurtures confident voices for positive change.

Debate League & Competitions Project

Through structured debate leagues and school competitions, students practice their skills in real environments, encouraging teamwork, leadership, discipline and intellectual confidence.

Teachers Debate Capacity Project

This project trains teachers as debate mentors, providing tools, methodologies and resources to guide students effectively and sustain debate culture within schools.

Youth Peace & Leadership Project

Using debate as a peacebuilding tool, this project promotes tolerance, respectful disagreement, ethical leadership and civic responsibility among young people.
