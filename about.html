About Our Organization

Empowering Youth through Debate, Public Speaking and Leadership

Organization Background

Our Story

The Youth Contest Beyond Vision is a youth-centered non-governmental organization founded to empower secondary school students through debating, public speaking, and leadership development programs.

The organization was initiated by passionate youth leaders who recognized that many young people possess ideas and talents but lack platforms to express them. Through structured debate training and intellectual competitions, the organization creates safe and inclusive environments for students to grow academically, socially, and ethically.

Since its establishment, the organization has focused on strengthening students' critical thinking, research abilities, communication skills, and confidence. Programs are conducted through schools, debate leagues, workshops, mentorship sessions, and collaborative educational partnerships.

How We Operate

The Youth Contest Beyond Vision operates through partnerships with schools, teachers, youth mentors, and community leaders. The organization delivers training sessions, organizes competitions, and promotes leadership initiatives that encourage peaceful dialogue and civic engagement among youth.

By combining mentorship, education, and practical competitions, the organization ensures students not only learn debate skills theoretically but also apply them in real-world leadership and communication environments.

Our Mission

We aim at improving critical thinking and communication skills of students so they become active citizens.

Through debate and public speaking training, we promote peaceful conflict resolution and leadership development.

We provide mentorship, competitions, and academic development programs.

Our Vision

To build a confident generation of leaders capable of influencing society through communication and ethical leadership.

We envision youth who can confidently express ideas, solve global challenges, and promote peace.
